ep 1: The Crybaby: Usagi's Beautiful Transformation

Usagi Tsukino is a 14-year-old schoolgirl who meets a talking black cat named Luna, who reveals that dark forces are threatening the lives of the innocent. Usagi is transformed into Sailor Moon, the chosen guardian who fights for love and justice.

ep 2: Punishment Awaits: The House Of Fortune Is The Monster Mansion

A new house of fortune opens up and quickly gains popularity for its accurate predictions. The customers who had their fortunes read begin to act violent. Suspecting that this is the work of a monster, Usagi and Luna decide to investigate the situation.

ep 3: The Mysterious Sleeping Sickness: Protect the Girls In Love

All the girls in town are tuning in to a late-night radio program that reads out love letters sent in by the listeners. Meanwhile, a strange sleeping sickness is plaguing the town that puts many people to sleep, including Ms. Haruda and Naru.

ep 4: Learn How to Be Skinny from Usagi

Usagi is shocked to find out that she’s gained weight from too much eating. Determined to get back into shape and fast, Usagi and her friends join a gym that advertises instant results for its members.

ep 6: Protect the Melody of Love: Usagi Plays Cupid

One rainy day, Usagi bumps into Yusuke Amade, a famous musician who claims he is being chased by a monster. Realizing the man is telling the truth, Usagi and Luna decide to keep an eye on Yusuke by staking out the venue where he performs.

ep 7: Usagi Learns Her Lesson: Becoming a Star Is Hard Work

Usagi and Naru both dream of becoming a famous star like Mikan Shiratori, a graduate from their school. When the girls hear that Mikan is hosting a talent contest, they see their chance to achieve stardom.

ep 8: The Girl Genius Is a Monster: The Brainwashing Cram School of Horror

Usagi befriends Ami Mizuno, the school’s smartest girl, who has an IQ over 300. But Luna, who senses an unknown energy coming from Ami, suspects her of being a Monster.

ep 9: Usagi's Disaster: Beware of the Clock of Confusion

Usagi and Ami go shopping, where they find a store selling a clock that resembles Luna. Usagi is happy to learn that her mother bought the same clock as a gift. That night, the clock emits a strange light that causes Usagi to become very impatient.

ep 10: The Cursed Bus: Enter Mars, the Guardian of Fire

Several people who take the bus near the Hikawa Shrine have started to go missing. Usagi and her friends decide to investigate by visiting the shrine, where they meet Rei Hino, a girl with strong spiritual powers.

ep 11: Usagi vs. Rei: Nightmare in Dream Land

The Sailor Guardians go to the Dream Land amusement park, where visitors have gone missing. The team splits into two groups with Rei pairing up with Usagi. But much to Rei’s frustration, Usagi is more focused on playing than finding the missing people.

ep 12: I Want a Boyfriend: The Luxury Cruise Ship is a Trap

Rei goes off on a luxury cruise with Ami after winning tickets through the local lottery. Feeling left behind, Usagi sneaks aboard the ship disguised as a photographer.

ep 13: Girls Unite: The End of Jadeite

Jadeite is given a final warning by Queen Beryl, who can no longer tolerate the general’s continual failures. Realizing this is his last chance, Jadeite challenges the Sailor Guardians to a showdown at the airport.

ep 14: A New Enemy Appears: Nephrite's Evil Crest

Rui is a talented tennis player at Usagi’s school, but Naru has noticed a dark change in her. Worried about their classmate, Naru and Usagi go to investigate at Rui’s tennis school.

ep 15: Usagi's Panic: Rei's First Date

Ami brings Usagi and Rei to her favorite park where the lake and peaceful environment make it a perfect dating spot. Unfortunately, the place is soon to be demolished. Rei decides to have a date with Mamoru at the park before it closes down.

ep 16: A Girl's Dream: Usagi Becomes a Bride

The Juban District is having a contest to decide who can design the best handmade wedding dress. Upon learning that the prize will be a free luxury wedding reception, Usagi, Rei, and all the girls in town compete to design their own wedding dresses.

ep 17: Usagi's a Model: The Flash of the Monster Camera

A young photographer named Kijin Shinokawa holds a modeling contest, and Usagi decides to enter. Mysteriously, all the girls who have entered the contest begin disappearing after Shinokawa photographs them!

ep 18: Shingo's Love: The Grieving Doll

Shingo’s friend Mika gives him a doll but Shingo unintentionally breaks the doll and hurts Mika’s feelings after being teased by boys at his school. Shingo decides to apologize, only to learn that Mika does not wish to see anyone.

ep 19: Usagi's Joy: A Love Letter from Tuxedo Mask

Usagi receives a love letter from Tuxedo Mask, stating that he’d like to meet her. But she soon finds out that all the girls in town received the same letter, including Naru, who believes that Tuxedo Mask is Masato Sanjoin.
